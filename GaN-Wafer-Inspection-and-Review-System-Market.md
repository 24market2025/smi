GaN Wafer Inspection and Review System Key Market Trends :
Increasing Demand for GaN in Power Electronics
The rising use of GaN in power devices for renewable energy and fast-charging applications is driving the need for precise wafer inspection systems.

Advancements in AI-Powered Defect Detection
AI and machine learning integration in inspection systems are improving defect identification, leading to higher yield and quality assurance.

Growing 5G and RF Semiconductor Production
The expansion of 5G and satellite communication networks is increasing the demand for GaN wafers, requiring advanced metrology solutions.

Adoption of Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Systems
Semiconductor manufacturers are increasingly investing in AOI systems for faster and more accurate GaN wafer defect detection.

Expansion of GaN-Based Devices in EVs and Aerospace
The use of GaN in electric vehicles, radar systems, and space applications is fueling the need for reliable inspection and quality control systems.
