Navigation Satellite Chip Market Overview
The navigation satellite chip is a highly integrated chip that integrates 32-channel GPS positioning, GSM voice, and GPRS data communications. Its function is to receive positioning information sent by navigation satellites. It is used in my country's Beidou satellite navigation and positioning system. As the core baseband processing chip of end-user computers, it can realize the integration of precise timing, navigation and positioning, remote control, voice communication and other functions.
