Simple Packaged Crystal Oscillator Market Overview
The simple package crystal oscillator is an oscillator that uses a quartz crystal as a frequency component and can generate a highly stable signal through a certain circuit connection and packaging process. This kind of oscillator is usually packaged in SMD (Surface Mounted Device), which has the characteristics of small size, light weight, and high precision. It is widely used in various electronic equipment, such as communication equipment, computers, instruments, etc.
