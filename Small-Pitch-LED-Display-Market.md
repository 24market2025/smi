Small Pitch LED Display Market Overview
he pitch of a LED screen defines the distance between the pixels. Small Pitch LED Display refers to the pixel pitch of LED Display which is below 2.5mm, including P2.5, P2.0, P1.8, P1.5 P 1.2, P 1, P 0.7, etc. With the development of LED display, it has been defined the pixel pitch below 2mm as the small pitch LED Display. In this report, we define the LED Display which the pixel pitch is below 2.5mm as the small pitch LED Display.

Growth in Smart Cities and Public Infrastructure
Governments worldwide are investing in smart city projects, boosting the demand for advanced LED display solutions for transportation hubs, command centers, and public venues.

Advancements in Mini & Micro-LED Technology
The continuous innovation in mini and micro-LED technology is enhancing display efficiency, brightness, and longevity, making them more suitable for diverse applications.

Surge in Adoption for Virtual & Hybrid Events
The rising popularity of virtual events, trade shows, and hybrid business models is propelling the demand for high-quality LED video walls and immersive display solutions.

Energy-Efficient and Sustainable Solutions
Manufacturers are focusing on energy-efficient LED displays with lower power consumption, aligning with global sustainability and eco-friendly initiatives.
